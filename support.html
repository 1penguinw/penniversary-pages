Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?

Everything you enter — occasions, gift and dinner entries, and photos — stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere. See our Privacy Policy for details.

I'm not getting reminder notifications. What should I check?

Make sure notifications are allowed for Penniversary in Settings → Notifications → Penniversary, and that "Notify me" is turned on for the specific occasion.

Will my data sync across my devices?

Not currently — data is stored locally on each device. Cross-device sync is something we're considering for a future update.

How do I delete an occasion or entry?

Swipe left on an item in the list, or open it and tap Edit, then Delete at the bottom of the form.

Can I recover data after deleting the app?

No — since all data lives only on your device, uninstalling the app removes it permanently.
